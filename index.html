About torrente

torrente street

Torrente (also called torrent in english)is a city in Valencia, Spain, that is part of the Valencia metropolitan region. With 83,962 residents, it is the largest municipality in the Horta Oest comarca (2020). It is around 7 kilometres from Valencia city centre, to which it is accessible by metro.

Torrent has an important historical and cultural history, as indicated by various regionally relevant festivals that draw thousands of tourists each year, and whose principal protagonist is the 12th century Tower.

HISTORY

moorish tower

Although the tower and its fortress were constructed many centuries earlier, the community of Torrent was formally created by individuals of European origin in 1248, during the Reconquista. According to archaeological study, the area was inhabited during the Bronze Age.

Eastern Spain's Valencia Comunidad Autónoma includes the city of Torrent, sometimes known as Torrente, in its east-central province. Southwest of Valencia City it is located. The Torre de Malta (Maltese Tower), a fortress with Moorish roots located in the heart of the city, is where the name Torrent originated. James I of Aragon gave the city to the Knights of St. John (becoming the Knights of Malta) in 1248, and they held onto it until 1807. A regional agricultural market exists. In Torrent, products including shoes, chocolate, and canned foods are made. 64,338 as of 2008 estimates.

Valencia is full of unexpected things, like the river that has been turned into a beautiful, 9-kilometer park that runs through the city and is perfect for all kinds of sports and leisure activities. A popular gathering place for Valencians and visitors, the River Turia's historic riverbed is accessible from dawn till night.

The beach in Port des Torrent is situated at the entrance to Sant Antoni Bay. This beach's name comes from the natural harbour it is located in. This harbour doubles as a neighbourhood and a well-liked tourism destination. Both visitors and residents may take peaceful baths and eat delicious meals in the neighbourhood.
